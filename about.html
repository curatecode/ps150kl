PS150 is a cocktail bar located at Petaling Street in the heart of China Town Kuala Lumpur. The building was built during the early 1900s and lived through different transformations and eras which continuously inspire us. At PS150, we serve signature cocktails from different eras with hints of oriental flavours specially designed by Angel Ng, our resident mixologist. A wide variety of spirits from all over the world, premium bottled beers and selection of wines are available for those who prefer to give our mixologists a break. Our tiny kitchen serves a snack menu that features ingredients available within China Town with the aim of perfecting simple local dishes using modernist techniques.
